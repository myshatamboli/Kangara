# Kangara

### Empowering Maternal Wellbeing Through Intelligent Monitoring

Kangara is a femtech venture developing a non-invasive wearable pregnancy monitoring system designed to provide reassurance, confidence, and support during late-stage pregnancy.

Inspired by the protective nature of kangaroos and the principles of kangaroo care, Kangara combines wearable technology and intelligent monitoring to help expectant mothers stay informed about their baby's wellbeing between clinical appointments.

---

## Our Mission

To empower mothers with greater confidence, comfort, and peace of mind throughout pregnancy by making wellbeing insights more accessible, understandable, and supportive.

---

## The Problem

Pregnancy can be one of the most exciting yet anxiety-inducing periods in a person's life.

Many expectant mothers experience uncertainty around:

- Fetal movement patterns
- Changes in wellbeing
- Contraction monitoring
- Knowing when medical attention may be needed
- Accessing reassurance between appointments

This can lead to unnecessary stress, uncertainty, and avoidable hospital visits.

---

## Our Solution

Kangara is developing a wearable pregnancy monitoring belt paired with a mobile application that helps mothers better understand patterns related to their pregnancy.

The system is designed to support, not replace, professional medical care.

Through intelligent monitoring and user-friendly insights, Kangara aims to help mothers feel more informed and supported throughout their pregnancy journey.

---

## Key Features

### Baby Movement Monitoring

Track and monitor fetal movement patterns to help mothers remain aware of changes over time.

### Heart Rate Trend Tracking

Monitor fetal heart rate trends and provide visibility into historical patterns.

### Contraction Monitoring

Support contraction tracking and timing during later stages of pregnancy.

### Mobile Application

A connected dashboard designed to provide accessible insights, alerts, and wellbeing information.

### Comfort-First Design

Designed using soft, breathable bamboo fabric with a focus on comfort, flexibility, and everyday wearability.

### Intelligent Alerts

Provide notifications when changes may require additional attention or professional guidance.

---

## Design Philosophy

Kangara was built around three principles:

### Comfort

Pregnancy technology should be supportive, lightweight, and comfortable.

### Accessibility

Mothers should have access to meaningful wellbeing information wherever they are.

### Empowerment

Technology should increase confidence and understanding, not create fear or confusion.

---

## Vision

We envision a future where every expectant mother feels supported, informed, and empowered throughout pregnancy.

By combining compassionate design with intelligent technology, Kangara aims to make maternal wellbeing more accessible for families everywhere.
